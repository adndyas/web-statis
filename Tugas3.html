1. Backburner

By NIKI

"Backburner" explores emotional backup relationships, where someone in a serious commitment maintains a backup chemistry, reflecting insecurity and emotional infidelity. This trend, accentuated during the pandemic, involves keeping options open in case the main relationship falters. A 2021 study found 62% of adults admitting to backburners, highlighting its prevalence despite committed relationships.
You can listen to the song here Backburner by NIKI



2. Alonica

By LANY

This song, "Alonica" by Lany, creates a melancholic vibe perfect for sleep because it portrays a bittersweet departure from Los Angeles (LA) to Alonica. The lyrics express falling out of love with LA and finding solace in Alonica's perpetual sunshine, favorite beach, and endless sand. The song captures a sense of longing, nostalgia, and the feeling of finding home in a place that brings comfort and peace. The soothing melody and poetic lyrics evoke a deep emotional connection, making it a great song for sleep.
You can listen to the song here Alonica by LANY



3. Always

By Daniel Caesar

This song, "Always" by Daniel Caesar, resonates as a perfect melancholic lullaby due to its heartfelt lyrics and soothing melody. The lyrics express a timeless love, with the speaker expressing devotion even if the relationship changes. Despite knowing the possibility of the partner being with someone else, the speaker pledges unwavering love and promises to always be there. The song captures the pain of longing, love, and the hope for enduring connection, making it a poignant choice for a melancholic mood.
You can listen to the song here Always by Daniel Caesar



4. Loving You

By Seafret

This Seafret track, "Loving You," encapsulates the feeling of sadness and lost love, making it ideal for bedtime. The song narrates a tale of love, strength, and embracing heartbreak. Its lyrics depict a previous relationship, recalling the warmth amidst a cold atmosphere, acknowledging the inevitable changes. The singer embraces the pain as a chapter of their life, expressing a profound, lasting love despite hurdles. The song's emotional intensity and relatable words create a comforting yet poignant lullaby, offering solace to those seeking peace in the night.
You can listen the song here Loving You by Seafret



5. A Year Ago

By James Arthur

This song hits deep for sleep because it captures heartbreak and longing. James Arthur's "A Year Ago" reminisces about lost love. He misses every little thing: the shared coffee cup, movie choices, and even the mess left behind. The lyrics express regret for not expressing love sooner. Arthur wishes he could turn back time, hold the person close, and convey his feelings. The song's melancholic tone and relatable lyrics make it perfect for sleep, evoking a sense of nostalgia and longing for lost love.
You can listen to the song A Year Ago
